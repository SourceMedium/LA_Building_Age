Building Age // 1890-2008

Since 2008, there are over 2.9 million buildings in the county. Each building is shaded according to the time they were built. Building Vector Tiles made with Mapbox Studio and Mapbox-GL by Omar Ureta, inspired by Portland, Oregon: The Age of a City Terrain and Label Data by Mapbox. OpenStreetMap, under ODbL.
